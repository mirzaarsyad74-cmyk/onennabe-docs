# Denuvo Games — Borrowing Account

Denuvo is a DRM (Digital Rights Management) system used by some games. These games require an extra step — you must borrow the seller's Steam account to activate the Denuvo licence on your machine before you can play from your own account.

!!! danger
    Denuvo games will **NOT work** without borrowing an account from your seller. You cannot skip this step.

---

## How to Identify a Denuvo Game

In the ONENNABE Store tab, Denuvo games will be tagged with the **`Denuvo`** label on the game card.

---

## Step-by-Step: Playing a Denuvo Game

1. Find the game in the Store tab — confirm it has the `Denuvo` tag
2. Click the game card → click **Enable Auto Update** in ONENNABE
3. Go to **Steam** and download the game normally
4. **Close Steam Unlock ONENNABE** — do this before the next step
5. Contact your seller and ask to borrow the account for your game
6. **Switch Steam** to the seller's account using the credentials they provide
7. Launch the game and play until you reach the **main menu**
8. **Switch Steam back** to your own account
9. Launch and play from your own account — enjoy!

---

## Important Rules

!!! warning "Close ONENNABE First"
    You must close ONENNABE **before** switching to the seller's account. If ONENNABE is still open, the process may not work correctly.

- **You must reach the main menu** on the seller's account — just launching the game is not enough. Denuvo requires a brief online validation session.
- **Switch back to your own account promptly** after reaching the main menu. Do not use the seller's account for anything else.
- **Each account borrow has a session limit.** Only use it to launch the game to the main menu.

---

## Exception — Resident Evil Requiem

!!! warning "Special Case"
    Resident Evil Requiem **cannot** be played by switching back to your own account. This game must be played entirely from the **seller's account in Steam Offline Mode**. Do NOT switch back to your own account for this title. Ask your seller for specific instructions.

---

## Troubleshooting

**Game says "not available" after switching back to my account**  
Make sure you reached the main menu on the seller's account before switching back. If you did, restart Steam and try again.

**Game crashes or shows a Denuvo error**  
The Denuvo session may have expired. Contact your seller to borrow the account again.

**Seller's account credentials not working**  
Contact your seller directly via the platform you purchased from (TikTok/Shopee chat or Discord DM).

**I forgot to close ONENNABE before switching accounts**  
Close ONENNABE now, switch to the seller's account, launch to main menu, switch back, then reopen ONENNABE.
