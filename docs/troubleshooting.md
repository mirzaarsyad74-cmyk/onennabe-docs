# Troubleshooting

If something is not working — patch not applying, game not launching, button missing — follow these steps **in order**. Try each fix before moving to the next.

---

## Fix 1 — Restart Your PC

The simplest fix and often the most effective.

1. Restart your PC fully
2. Let Windows finish booting completely
3. Open Steam and wait for it to fully load
4. Open ONENNABE and try again

---

## Fix 2 — Restart Steam

1. Close Steam completely (right-click the Steam tray icon → **Exit**)
2. Wait a few seconds
3. Reopen Steam and log in
4. Reopen ONENNABE and try again

---

## Fix 3 — Clear Steam Download Cache

1. Open **Steam Unlock ONENNABE**
2. Go to the **Tools** section
3. Click **Clear Steam Download Cache**
4. Wait for it to complete
5. Steam will restart — log back in
6. Try again

---

## Fix 4 — Reinstall Steam (via ONENNABE Tools)

If all the above did not help, use the Reinstall Steam option built into ONENNABE. This does a clean Steam reinstall without affecting your downloaded games.

1. Open **Steam Unlock ONENNABE**
2. Go to the **Tools** section
3. Click **Reinstall Steam**
4. Wait for the process to complete
5. **Re-login to Steam** with your account credentials after it finishes
6. Reopen ONENNABE — activate with your CD Key if prompted
7. Try again

!!! warning "Have Your Login Ready"
    After reinstalling Steam, you **must log back in**. Have your Steam username and password ready before starting this step.
