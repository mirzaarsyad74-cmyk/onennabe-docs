# CD Key Activation

Your CD Key is what activates ONENNABE and gives you access to the game catalogue. You receive this from your seller after purchase.

---

## How to Activate
<img width="989" height="1040" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/00f73e14-a42e-4f3d-ae24-1e2d8097dd0d" />

1. Make sure **Steam is running** and you are logged in
2. Open **Steam Unlock ONENNABE**
3. The activation screen will appear automatically on first launch
4. Enter your CD Key **exactly as provided** — it is case-sensitive
5. Click **Activate**
6. Wait for the key to be verified
7. Your game library will appear after successful activation

## Key Rules

- **One key = one Steam account.** Your key is tied to the Steam account you activate it on.
- **Never share your key.** Sharing it will cause it to be deactivated.
- **Keys are case-sensitive.** Copy and paste the key if possible to avoid typos.

---

## Troubleshooting

**"License invalid" error**  
Double-check the key for typos. Make sure Steam is running and you are online. If copied from a message, check for extra spaces before or after the key.

**Key was working before but now shows invalid**  
Restart Steam and reopen ONENNABE. If the issue persists, contact your seller.

**Key not provided by seller yet**  
If you bought via TikTok Shop, the parcel must arrive first. For Shopee or other platforms, DM your seller directly in the purchase chat.

**Activated on wrong Steam account**  
Contact your seller to request a key transfer.
