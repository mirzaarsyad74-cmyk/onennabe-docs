# Downloading ONENNABE

Before downloading, you must turn off **Real-Time Protection** in Windows Defender. If you skip this step, the installer will be deleted automatically as soon as it finishes downloading.

![Defender Warning](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3138d0d7-abc2-491f-a047-043b6603836c)

---

## Step 1 — Turn Off Real-Time Protection

[🛡️ Open Real-Time Protection Settings](ms-settings:windowsdefender)

*Note: Click "Allow" or "Open Settings" if your browser prompts you.*

> **If the button above is blocked by your browser:**
> Press **Win + R**, paste `ms-settings:windowsdefender`, and press **Enter**.
> 
> ![Run Dialog](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7e3fa3ed-5e95-4d4e-a3f5-d4612c98d53a)

![Windows Security Overview](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/64f20798-ecb5-437a-8401-d2c88127bc1f)

1. Open **Windows Security** (search for it in the Start menu).
    
    ![Search Windows Security](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b98555b6-81e7-422e-aa38-855b38d7eb27)

2. Click **Virus & threat protection**.
    
    ![Virus & threat protection](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e4eef296-4b16-407e-ab73-ccd6b2284132)

3. Click **Manage settings** under "Virus & threat protection settings".
    
    ![Manage settings](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/424b976b-3d67-433a-acd3-902b8e9c83ef)

4. Toggle **Real-time protection** to **Off**.

!!! warning
    Keep Real-Time Protection **off** until after ONENNABE is fully installed. You can turn it back on once installation is complete, but make sure you have added the exclusion folders first — see [Installation](installation.md).

## Step 2 — Download via Google Chrome

Watch the video below for a full walkthrough of the download process:

### Chrome Download Steps

1. Open **Google Chrome**.
2. Go to the ONENNABE download link provided by your seller.
3. Click the download button.
4. If Chrome shows a warning saying **"This file may be harmful"**, choose one of the methods below:

    **Method 1: Direct Prompt**
    
    ![Chrome Warning Prompt 1](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d2fa1705-9b18-4a10-a1d0-58a9660f8056)
    
    * Click the **arrow (⌄)** next to the discard button.
    * Click **Keep** or **Keep anyway**.

    **Method 2: Downloads Page**
    
    ![Chrome Warning Prompt 2](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8402457f-0a91-4525-9454-1f2c9981d078)
    
    * On your Keyboard, press **CTRL + J** to open the Downloads page.
    * Find the downloaded file.
    * Click the three dots (**⋮**) next to it.
    * Click **Keep** → then **Keep anyway**.

5. Wait for the download to complete.
6. Open the downloaded installer.

!!! tip "Why Chrome?"
    Chrome handles the "Keep anyway" prompt more reliably than other browsers for unsigned executables. Edge may block the file entirely without an option to keep it.
